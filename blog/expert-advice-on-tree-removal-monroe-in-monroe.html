When Is Tree Removal Necessary?

Several situations warrant professional tree removal:

  • Dead or dying trees showing significant decay
  • Trees with severe storm damage
  • Trees growing too close to structures or power lines
  • Disease-infected trees that can't be saved
  • Trees with compromised root systems

Signs Your Monroe Property Needs Tree Removal

If you notice any of these warning signs, it's time to consider professional removal:

  • Visible root damage or exposed roots
  • Leaning trunk (especially if recently developed)
  • Multiple dead branches
  • Fungal growth around the base
  • Cracked or peeling bark
